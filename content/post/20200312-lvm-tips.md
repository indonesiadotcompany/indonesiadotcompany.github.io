## resize / increase lvm
```
lvextend --size +10G /dev/vg0/foo
lvextend --size 20G /dev/vg0/foo
lvdisplay /dev/vg0/foo
```

* http://www.microhowto.info/howto/increase_the_size_of_an_lvm_logical_volume.html
